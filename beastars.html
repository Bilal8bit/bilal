Beastars

Beastars is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Paru Itagaki. The story is set in a world of anthropomorphic animals and follows a gray wolf named Legoshi, who is a member of the drama club at Cherryton Academy. The series explores themes of prejudice, identity, and societal roles as it delves into the complex relationships between carnivores and herbivores.

The manga has been praised for its unique art style, compelling characters, and thought-provoking narrative. It has won several awards, including the Manga Taisho Award and the Kodansha Manga Award. Beastars was also adapted into an anime television series, which further increased its popularity.
